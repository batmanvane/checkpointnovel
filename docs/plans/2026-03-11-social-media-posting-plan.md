# Social Media Posting Plan

## Post 1: Launch Announcement (LinkedIn)

**Attachment:** Animated timeline clip (marketing/clips/social-media-clip.mov)

**Text:**

Between semesters, I usually finish a draft paper. This time it was a novel within a few days.

I work with machine learning in applied engineering research — agentic workflows, autonomous systems, the usual. I took that same approach and pointed it at fiction. The result: a complete hard sci-fi novel, an audiobook, a website, and a social media campaign. From zero to published.

The novel is called *Checkpoint*. It's set in 2041. A brain-computer interface — a small patch behind the ear — has been adopted by 900 million people. It reads the brain. It also writes to it. Four lives at the edges of what it means to think for yourself.

It was co-written with Claude (Anthropic's AI). The process raised one of several questions the book asks: where does the machine stop and the human start?

The whole thing is free under Creative Commons. Explore the work and repo at https://checkpoin.de

Consider it a case study: what happens when you take agentic workflows from coding and science and apply them to creative work? I'm genuinely curious what people think — about the text, the process, the implications. Leave a comment if you have thoughts.
